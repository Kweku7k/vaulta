Client onboarding

Thank you for choosing to partner with us.

We are pleased to move forward and look forward to a successful collaboration. As part of our standard onboarding process and in preparation for execution, we kindly request your assistance in providing the following compliance documentation.

Typically issued by the Registrar of Companies or equivalent authority.

Full, signed copy of your current Memorandum and Articles of Association.

Please provide details of all Ultimate Beneficial Owners (UBOs) holding more than 10% of the business.

Include full names, ownership percentages and, where available, identification numbers for all individuals holding more than 10% of the business.

Upload a certified company profile including an overview of the management team and organisational structure.

Include key management biographies and a clear organisation chart.

Upload certified copies of valid passports and proof of residential address (e.g. utility bill dated within the last 3 months) for key directors and shareholders.

You may bundle multiple IDs into a single PDF or ZIP file.

Upload a certified proof of the company address, such as a utility bill or similar document issued within the last 3 months.

Ensure the document clearly shows the company name and registered address.

If your business is regulated, upload the name of the regulatory body and a copy of the operating licence or letter of no objection (LONO).

Optional — only where a regulatory licence or LONO applies.

Upload the latest 3 months’ bank statements or a statement of good standing not older than 6 months to evidence the source of funds.

You may provide statements from multiple accounts in a single combined PDF or compressed archive.

Complete a quick identity verification to confirm your details. You will be guided through a secure process powered by Persona. Please have a valid government-issued ID ready.

By submitting these documents you confirm that, to the best of your knowledge, the information provided is accurate, complete and up to date. Our team will review the documentation and contact you if any clarification is required.
